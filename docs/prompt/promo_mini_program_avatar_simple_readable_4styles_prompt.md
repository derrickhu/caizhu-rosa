# 彩珠五连小程序头像：简洁高识别 4 风格

参考方向：借鉴成熟小游戏头像的做法，一个明确主体 + 高对比背景 + 粗轮廓 + 少量大形状。不要做复杂宣传图，不要满屏碎细节。

```text
Create a single 2x2 grid sheet containing four different square WeChat mini-game app icon concepts for 彩珠五连, a colorful five-in-a-row marble puzzle game.

Use the provided fruit-bowl icon reference ONLY for icon design quality: simple high-contrast background, one strong readable subject, thick clean outline, cute polished 2D mobile-game illustration, clear at 48px. Do NOT copy the bowl, fruits, pineapple, or food theme.

Overall icon art direction:
- Simple, attractive, high readability at 48px and 64px.
- 2D hand-painted cartoon mobile game icon, polished but not over-rendered.
- Strong thick dark teal / navy outlines around main shapes.
- Bright distinct background color fills the full square edge to edge.
- No white border, no pale outside margin, no transparent padding, no rounded-square tile on white.
- Use only a few large marbles and simple shapes; avoid clutter, tiny confetti, tiny bubbles, complex effects, many small balls.
- Clearly communicate the game: five colorful glass marbles connecting in a line / five-in-a-row puzzle board.
- Each icon must be complete, centered, and safe for circular crop.
- NO TEXT, no Chinese characters, no English letters, no numbers, no labels, no watermark, no logo, no QR code, no fake glyphs.

Grid layout:
2 columns x 2 rows, equal square cells, thin clean gutters between cells.

Top-left cell: Five Marble Line Hero
Solid turquoise-to-deep-aqua gradient background with a soft pale circular halo. Exactly five large glossy marbles connect in one slightly diagonal rising line from lower-left to upper-right, all same blue color with bright white highlights. Add one simple golden glow behind the line. The five-marble line fills 80% of icon width. Very clean, iconic, instantly readable.

Top-right cell: Mini Puzzle Board Icon
Deep royal blue background with a simple cyan halo. A small rounded square puzzle board fills about 78% of icon width, thick navy outline, only a 5x5 grid suggested with very few visible grid lines. In the center row, exactly five red glossy marbles connect horizontally, large and touching. Board and five marbles are the only focus.

Bottom-left cell: Rainbow Wild Marble Badge
Vivid purple-to-indigo background with a soft aqua halo. One oversized rainbow glossy marble at center, surrounded by exactly four smaller colorful marbles at north/south/east/west, forming a simple five-dot flower/star. Thick outline, clean silhouette, minimal sparkle only two or three large highlights. Premium app icon feeling.

Bottom-right cell: Ocean Bubble Classic
Bright cyan background with a simple dark teal circular badge. Five yellow glossy marbles form a clean horizontal line across the badge, with one small white clearing shine behind them. Add only 3 or 4 large bubbles, no small busy particles. Friendly casual puzzle icon, bold and simple.

Strict constraints:
- Generate exactly four square icon concepts in a 2x2 grid.
- Every cell must have a full-bleed colored background to the edge.
- Each cell must be visually separable and usable as a standalone app icon.
- Do NOT include a white outer border or white canvas around any icon.
- Do NOT draw a raw gameplay screenshot.
- No text of any kind.
```
